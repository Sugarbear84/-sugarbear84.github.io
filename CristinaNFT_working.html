Il Nodo

Il Nodo is the violin and piano duet from Ex Voto—a collection of nine pieces for piano, cello, and violin. I wanted to create music that is more personal and exposed than anything else that I have written. The inspiration for this work came from a visit to the Cloisters in New York and from my Catholic, Italian-American upbringing. The term “ex voto” comes from the Latin ex voto suscepto, “from the vow made” and is an offering to a saint or divinity given in fulfillment of a vow in gratitude or devotion. Il Nodo was recorded at Creative Workshop in Nashville, TN with violinist Christina McGann. A collaborative classical work preserved digitally.
